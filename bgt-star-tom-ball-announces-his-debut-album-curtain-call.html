Britain’s Got Talent star Tom Ball has announced the upcoming release of his debut album, titled Curtain Call. The singer shared on Friday that the album is now available for pre-order. Ball has also released a new single.

Tom Ball Announces Debut Album ‘Curtain Call’

“What an incredible year this has been! Everything I’ve been building towards over this last year is finally happening and none of it would be possible without you,” Ball wrote in the caption of his Instagram post announcing that pre-order is open for the album.

In a video, Ball shared that those who pre-order the album will receive his new single, which is a cover of “I (Who Have Nothing)” by Tom Jones. They will also receive three bonus tracks, including Ball’s cover of “Sound of Silence” by Simon & Garfunkel.

According to the website for pre-order, the album’s cover and track listing will be revealed soon. Ball also has yet to announce an official release date for the project, but the website lists November 3.

Ball’s new single, “I (Who Have Nothing)” is a studio version of a cover he performed on BGT Series 15. He’s previously released covers of “Creep,” “Falling Slowly,” and “Sound of Silence.” All of these tracks show off Ball’s soaring vocals.

Ball Reached the ‘AGT: All-Stars’ Finals

Ball placed third during Series 15 of Britain’s Got Talent. During his audition, he shared that he was a school teacher before delivering a stunning performance of “Writing’s on the Wall” by Sam Smith.

Earlier this year, Ball brought his talents stateside to compete on America’s Got Talent: All-Stars. The singer earned the group Golden Buzzer from the judges for his performance of “Sound of Silence,” sending him to the Finals. Recently, Ball competed on Got Talent All-Stars in Spain, although he didn’t reach the Finals.
