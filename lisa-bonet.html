Lisa Bonet (Actress) - On This Day

Publish date: 2024-05-20

Full Name: Lisa Michelle Bonet
Profession: Actress

Biography: Most well known professionally for her role in the phenomenally successful sitcom "The Cosby Show" alongside Bill Cosby and Phylicia Rashad which ran from 1984-1992. She also appeared in its spin-off "A Different World".

Bonet was married to the singer-songwriter Lenny Kravitz from 1987-1993 and fellow actor Jason Momoa from 2017-2022. She is the mother of actress and model Zoë Kravitz.
