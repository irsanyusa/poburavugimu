Johnny Bench (MLB Catcher) - On This Day

Publish date: 2024-06-30

Profession: MLB Catcher

Biography: Bench played in the MLB for 16 years with the Cincinnati Reds.

A 14-time All-Star selection and a two-time National League Most Valuable Player, he was a key member of The Big Red Machine, which won six division titles, four National League pennants, and two World Series championships.

He is widely considered to be the best catcher in MLB history.
