Gwen Stefani Set to Headline The TikTok Tailgate at Super Bowl 2024

Publish date: 2024-08-14

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by: Tyler Golden/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Pop icon Gwen Stefani is set to headline the 2024 TikTok Tailgate, the pregame event preceding football’s biggest night. Live from Las Vegas, the vocal powerhouse will perform a 50-minute set for the massive crowd. Portions will even air on CBS’s Super Bowl pregame coverage.

Gwen Stefani to Headline Her First Super Bowl Show

For Stefani, performing at such a globally viewed event represents a monumental career milestone. The coveted gig further cements Stefani’s position as an unstoppable force in pop culture decades into her career.

A three-time Grammy winner, Stefani first hit mega-fame fronting ska-punk band No Doubt in the 1990s with anthems like “Just a Girl” and “Don’t Speak.” Her 2004 solo smash “Hollaback Girl” became an instant pop classic. Since then, she’s notched multiple number one hits spanning pop, dance, and country collaborations while captivating fans with her bold style.

Stefani remains an influencer and fixture in pop culture. So it makes perfect sense that she would take the TikTok Tailgate stage, given her massive following. With over three million fans on the platform, she’s tailor-made for creating viral and also engaging content that draws in younger generations.

Expect a high-octane show featuring smash hits old and new, dazzling costumes, and also Stefani’s signature spunk and spirit. The fearless fashion pioneer seems destined to deliver a high-energy spectacle perfect for sharing on social media. Her TikTok Tailgate performance certainly has the potential to be a career-defining moment.

How to Watch Stefani’s TikTok Tailgate Show

Stefani will perform at the Super Bowl 2024 TikTok Tailgate at Allegiant Stadium in Las Vegas on Sunday, February 11, 2024.

Fans around the world can tune in for free on TikTok starting at 1 PM PST. The pregame celebration features top TikTok creators before Stefani’s 50-minute set.

Portions of her performance will be televised on the CBS Super Bowl LVIII Pregame Show leading up to the big game’s kickoff at 3:30 PM PST.

In a statement, Stefani said: “I am so honored to perform at the Super Bowl TikTok Tailgate. Can’t wait to see you all in Vegas!”

On Location has limited Super Bowl 2024 ticket packages starting at $9,000. Resale prices average around $6,500.
