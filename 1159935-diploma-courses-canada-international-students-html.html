Publish date: 2024-06-08

Canada is one of the most highly developed and safe countries in the world. Checkout various diploma courses in Canada. International diploma certificate can open doors to an even brighter future for you.

Higher education in Canada for international students

Millions of applicants from around the world are eager to become students of one of Canada's higher education institutions. The popularity of studying in this country is caused by the high standard of living, the minimum indicators of crime and, most importantly, the system of higher education that is ideal in all respects.

There are so many Master's courses in Canada, as well as diploma and certification programs; English courses; work programs and internships for international students in Canada.

The admission to Canadian universities is possible immediately after school graduation in Nigeria, you only need to pass the English language test. A Canadian state certificate or a diploma can be obtained for a period of one to four years, while the price of studying, as well as the total cost of living in this country - are among the most attractive among English-speaking countries.

Since June 2014, Canadian legislation has become more friendly for foreign students in Canada - international students have the right to work while studying without an additional permit.
