American Idol finalist and academy award winning actress Jennifer Hudson shared an adorable photo of her son and his friends clearly proud of her role in Respect, an upcoming biopic about Aretha Franklin. Hudson shared a photo on social media of her son in front of the movie poster.

Hudson is making big waves once again in the music and film industries with the new film. Respect premieres in theaters on August 13, and fans cannot wait to see her in the iconic role of Aretha Franklin.

With this upcoming film’s premiere date on the horizon, many theaters have begun putting the poster in their lobbies. It seems like Hudson’s son saw the poster when in the lobby of a theater:

“Awwhhh when your kid goes to the movies and see mommy poster!!!” said Hudson in the post.

Hudson’s son, David Otunga Jr., whom she shares with ex-fiancé, David Otunga, can be seen in the back of Hudson’s post holding what looks to be a slushy. Fans who might remember the birth of Hudson’s son were quick to comment on how grown up he looks.

Yes, Jennifer Hudson Has a Son

Hudson’s son is turning 12-years-old in August. Although Hudson doesn’t post many photos with her son, she does post about him whenever she can. The pics are always super cute, you call tell she’s a proud mom!

The resemblance between the two is uncanny! Hudson and her son look super cute in these photos. We love to see Ortunga supporting his mom by posing by the movie poster with friends.
