The Voice Season 21 artist Libianca is giving fans everything they wanted by releasing a full cover of Billie Eilish’s “Everything I Wanted,” which she performed in the Knockouts on Team Blake. Libianca made it to the Top 20, but was eliminated after the first live show.

Libianca Releases ‘Everything I Wanted’ Cover

Libianca went up against Hailey Green on Team Blake in the Knockouts this past season. She performed a soulful rendition of “Everything I Wanted,” winning the Knockout and keeping her place on Blake Shelton’s team. Fans are excited to finally have a full version of the song.

“I love spoiling y’all cuz y’all are my babies,” Libianca wrote on Instagram to announce the cover. She even added a little inside joke to the end of the track. When the song is over, you might be a little confused to hear Libianca say, “I just farted.”

As the singer explained on Instagram, that joke “wasn’t my idea,” but rather “some of y’all’s.” It’s certainly an interesting way to end the song, but Libianca really does give her fans what they want, and they’re loving her for it.

Fans Are Loving the New Cover

Fans weren’t shy about sharing how much they loved Libianca’s new cover. As one YouTube commenter wrote, “Waited for this since the knockouts. My favourite performance from the whole season. It’s just magical.” Another commenter called her talent “unreal.”

Now fans are asking the singer to release full versions of even more songs she performed on the show, such as SZA’s “Good Days,” which she sang in the Blind Auditions, and Doja Cat’s “Woman,” which she sang in the Top 20.

However, Libianca is also focused on her own music these days. She has a song out called “Special Lovin’,” and she released a music video for the track earlier this month. You can keep up with her future releases by following her on Instagram.
