Scott Weiland (Rocker and Singer-Songwriter)

Publish date: 2024-05-26

Profession: Rocker and Singer-Songwriter

Biography: Weiland is Best known as the two-time lead singer of the Stone Temple Pilots (1989-2002, 2008-13).

He was also a member of Velvet Revolver (2003-08), as well as having a solo and collaborative career before his tragic death.

Born: October 27, 1967
Birthplace: San Jose, California, USA

Generation: Generation X
Chinese Zodiac: Goat/Sheep
Star Sign: Scorpio

Died: December 3, 2015 (aged 48)
Cause of Death: Accidental drug overdose of cocaine, alcohol and methylenedioxyamphetamine (MDA)
