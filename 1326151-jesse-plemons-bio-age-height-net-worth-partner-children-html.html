Early life and family

Jesse Lon Plemons was born on 2nd April in 1988 to Jim Bob Plemons and Lisa Beth (nee Cason). The actor was born in Dallas, Texas, as the second child of his parents. His elder sister goes by the name Jill.

At the moment, Jesse Plemons age is 32 years. The actor is of mixed ancestry, that is, English, Scottish, Scots-Irish/Northern Irish, and Swiss German.

Jesse grew up partly in Mart, Texas, and partly in Los Angeles due to his budding career as a young entertainer.

For his education, he went to Mart High School. Jesse earned his G.E.D when he was 16 years old. He graduated three years later, in 2007, through a long-distance learning program offered by the Texas Technical University Independent School District.

The actor also took acting classes from the Dallas Young Actors Studio in Dallas, Texas.

Jesse’s first role was for a Coca-Cola advertisement. He was barely four years old at the time. While he was young, his mother took him to auditions, and this is what led to the actor landing roles.
