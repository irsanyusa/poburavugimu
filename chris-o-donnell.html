Chris O'Donnell (Actor) - On This Day

Publish date: 2024-07-02

Profession: Actor

Biography: Chris O'Donnell is an American actor who came to fame in the 1992 film "Scent of a Woman" with Al Pacino. Following this he landed the role of DC comic character Robin in movies "Batman Forever" (1995) and "Batman & Robin" (1997).

O'Donnell then moved into TV roles, earning guest parts before landing the lead in CBS drama "NCIS LA" playing G. Callen alongside LL Cool J.
