20+ Readers Shared Their Kids Drawings, and Our Illustrators Added Just a Touch of Magic

Publish date: 2024-08-12

If there’s anything that truly defines childhood, it’s the way in which kids can imagine and put their thoughts “on paper” by using their most valuable skill: unlimited creativity. Kids merely need a few coloring pencils and a piece of paper to come up with something amazing, bringing some of the greatest ideas into the world. It’s no wonder Picasso used to say that it took him 4 years to paint like Raphael but a lifetime to paint like a child.

With that in mind, we asked Bright Side readers to share their children’s favorite drawings. We then went to the illustration department and challenged them to add a touch of magic to the best drawings that readers sent us. Here are the results and all of them are amazing. Just take a look below.
