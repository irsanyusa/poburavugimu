Kourtney Kardashian gave her niece Chicago West a mini neon Mercedes G-Wagon to match Kim Kardashian

Publish date: 2024-08-24

Chicago, who Kim and Kanye West welcomed via surrogate in January 2018, was given a mini neon Mercedes G-Wagon by her aunt, Kourtney Kardashian . Kim took to her Instagram story on Tuesday to show off the vehicle, which includes seats for passengers, fully functioning headlights, and a speaker system. It also closely resembles a full-sized neon G-Wagon that Kanye gave Kim a few months ago.

Here's a closer look at the Mercedes G-Wagon, which was reposted by a fan account.

Chicago looked thrilled to be behind the wheel, as seen in another video of the 1 year old with Kanye and the couple's two older children, daughter North (born in 2013) and son Saint (born in 2015).

It's unclear where Kourtney found the mini vehicle, but a similar model (without the upgrades) called the Mercedes Benz G500 by Autoart is available for $225.90 and sold by Target and Walmart .

Back in August, Kanye surprised Kim with her own Mercedes after the couple rented one during a vacation in Florida .

"I just got out of the gym and was surprised by this new baby," Kim said in a video she shared online. "I loved it so much in Miami, and I kept on talking about it."
