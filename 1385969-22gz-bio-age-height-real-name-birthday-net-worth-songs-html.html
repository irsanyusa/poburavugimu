22Gz bio: age, height, real name, birthday, net worth, songs

22Gz is an American rap artist who gained fame after his hit single Suburban blew up online. He is best known for his rap style and drill music, where he focuses his lyrics on crime and the daily ordeals of life on the streets. 22Gz is regarded as an important figure in Brooklyn's rapidly emerging drill scene.

Here is all you need to know about 22Gz:

Profile summary

  • Stage name: 22Gz, Tutu Blicky
  • Date of birth: November 29, 1997
  • Place of birth: Brooklyn, New York
  • Ethnicity: Guyanese
  • Occupation: Rapper

What is 22Gz real name?

The star's real name is Jeffrey Alexander. He is a native of Nostrand Avenue's 1800 block in Flatbush, Brooklyn, New York. The 22 in his stage named is pronounced "tutu," which refers to 2 Lane Avenue, the New Orleans address where his parents discovered they were pregnant with him.

22Gz music

The star hit song Suburban has amassed over 1.8 million views on YouTube as of November 2020. He is credited for pioneering the Brooklyn drill scene, and Suburban is arguably a key turning point that has seen the genre grow. Some have described 22Gz songs as a synthesis of aggressive flows, brutal lyricism with a Brooklyn slang.

After beating his murder charges, the rapper has focused solely on his music. His singles Blixky and Suburban cast him in the lime-light as he has received mainstream hip-hop attention. In 2018, 22Gz attracted the attention of Kodak Black, who signed him to his Florida-based label and Atlantic subsidiary, Sniper Gang.
