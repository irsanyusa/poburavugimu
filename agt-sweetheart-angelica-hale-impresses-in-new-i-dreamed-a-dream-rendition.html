Angelica Hale sure knows how to fascinate the internet with her powerful theatrical voice. The America’s Got Talent singer has recently treated fans with a fantastic rendition of Lea Salonga’s Les Miserables classic, “I Dreamed A Dream.”

Angelica Hale Records Raw “I Dreamed a Dream” Rendition

Former America’s Got Talent stand out Angelica Hale has officially taken the plunge into the world of musical theater. In her most recent Instagram post, the singer admitted that she’s been into singing operatic songs lately. Hale, who’s known to be generous to fans when it comes to covers, just had to share one.

“Recently, i’ve been listening to a lot of broadway and disney songs, and one of my favorites is Lea Salonga’s version of ‘I Dreamed a Dream.’ I wanted to sing a little bit for you guys!” she wrote.

Wearing a white tank top, and with little to no makeup, Hale powerfully belted out the song. The singer was obviously passionate while singing the piece, manifested in her emotions in the video. Fans are impressed with this strong rendition. So much that they fled to the comment section to praise the young songstress.

“Absolutely beautiful. And acapella in a hotel room on a cell phone. Want to hear this on a stage with a full orchestra backing you up,” one fan wrote.

“Angelica your voice makes my soul explode with so much emotion,” another fan wrote.

Fans hope to see a full version of this cover posted to YouTube in the near future. Back in September, Hale recorded “Let The Light In” by Lana Del Rey. While recording this video, Hale explained her obsession with the single. Even though she was sick at the time, she still recorded a cover of it.

Angelica Hale Joins BOOP! The Musical

It’s no wonder Hale has been loving Broadway songs these days. The AGT sweetheart will be a part of the upcoming BOOP! The Musical.

For the upcoming musical, Hale will be paying the character of Trisha, Betty Boop’s friend. Last week, she revealed that she’s already moved to New York City for the role. She’s also posted a series of snaps related to the musical.

“Boop-oop-a doop! trisha is here! ❣️ what an honor it is to be apart of such an incredible project, i am so grateful and thrilled to be a part of this journey. can’t wait for you guys to see the show, we have something AMAZING in store for you all!!” she wrote.

Jasmine Amy Rogers will star in the title role. Rogers will also be joined by Faith Prince as Valentina, Ainsley Melham as Dwayne, Erich Bergen as Raymond, Stephen DeRosa as Grampy, and Anastacia McCleskey as Carol. The musical will also feature music by Grammy winner David Foster.

“That sassy-sweet champion of empowerment, that spit-curled icon of joy, that songstress of strength comes alive in BOOP!, the new Broadway-bound musical-comedy extravaganza,” its official description reads.

The new musical will play a five-week engagement beginning November 19 at the CIBC Theatre in Chicago.
